Product

Mink blankets, commonly referred to as “faux mink” blankets or “faux fur blankets", are not what their name implies. They have nothing to do with real mink fur or any other fur for that matter. The typical make-up of a mink blanket or throw blanket is 85% acrylic and l5% polyester. The acrylic supplies the “softness” while the polyester keeps the mink blanket or throw blanket from wrinkling.

Our collection of elegant blankets is a perfect match to the contemporary room decor. These blankets are luxuriously soft and are cosy to the core. The subtle colour scheme give these blankets a rich look. Available in standard sizes, these blankets require no special care. Our range of elegant blankets can be availed at the most competitive prices.
