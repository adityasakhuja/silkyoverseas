About Us

Silky Overseas is all about details, sharp design and effortless style


Silky has its factory in Panipat, India spread over an area of 4 acres with a constructed area of 80,000 square feet. Our manufacturing unit boasts state-of-the-art machinery based on the advanced technology from China. We have streamlined facilities for knitting, dyeing, processing, printing, finishing rand packaging. The complete facility is backed by the team of highly skilled and experienced workforce, who ensure zero defects at every stage of production.
